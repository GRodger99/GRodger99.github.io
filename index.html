Hoard Hunter (GMTK-2024 Game Jam)

In 2024 with some Colleagues from Splash, I participated in the GMTK GameJam with the theme of "Built To Scale".
The game is unfinished (Such be the nature of a gamejam and last minute team forming) but revolves around taking treasure from the dragons den without waking it up!

I implemented a simple State based Music system that reacts to when the dragon wakes up, and added some quick library sounds I found online for gameplay (I'm no designer! But I'll implement them!).
Alongside this I scaled the volume of the dragon sleeping and some rumbling thresholds to help alert the player to a rising "Awakeness" of the dragon.
This was actually my first game Jam and I had a genuine blast, please take a look if you have some time!
